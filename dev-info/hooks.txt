--- Hooks available (by default) ---
* All events: They all have #start (called before any code) and #end (called after any code), for example: beforeChatMessage#start, afterChangeTeam#end, serverStartUp#start, etc.
  Look in scripts/events.js for a list of events.
  Exceptions: step, beforeNewMessage, afterNewMessage (for performance)

--- Hook IDs used (by default) ---
* ifyAfterLogIn#start
* ifyBeforeLogIn#start
* ifyAfterChangeTeam#end
