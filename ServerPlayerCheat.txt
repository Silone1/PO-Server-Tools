Cheating PO Registry Players:
First, download the PO source using Git.

Change src/Server/server.cpp
Line 536, function regSendPlayers:
    registry_connection->notify(NetworkServ::ServNumChange, quint16(AntiDos::obj()->numberOfDiffIps()));
to
    int cheatcode = (::rand()%(151-40)) + 40;
	registry_connection->notify(NetworkServ::ServNumChange, quint16(AntiDos::obj()->numberOfDiffIps()+cheatcode));

Rands additional amount of players
min 40, maximum 150:
151 = maximum + 1
40 = minimum
Simply follow ^that to change amount of extra players displayed.

Then compile PO in this order:
src/Utilities src/PokemonInfo src/Server
along with any plugins. (optional, you can copy paste plugin files from offical
po compile in serverdir/plugins!)
